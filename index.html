Atri Mandal

Senior Research Engineer, IBM Research
Email: atri[dot]mandal[at]gmail[dot]com


Atri Mandal received his Masters degree in Information and Computer Science from University of California, Irvine(USA) in 2004 and is currently employed as a Senior Research Engineer at IBM Research,India . He has over 17 years of experience in software development and research and has worked on many interesting and complex research problems in the areas of Log Analytics, AIOps, Machine Learning and NLP.

His current research work in AIOps focuses on the problem of localizing operational and software faults by mining distributed execution logs from micro-services in a cloud-native application. He has also extensively worked on related problems like anomaly detection/root cause analysis from logs, natural language based ticket classification and incident similarity. His work on “Automated Dispatch of Helpdesk Email Tickets” won the Deployed Paper Award at IAAI-2019 (Hawaii, USA). He has 10 peer-reviewed publications in top-tier journals and conferences and has filed 8 patents with IBM so far.

Outside his regular work-related activities Atri is very passionate about food and travel. He is also an ardent cricket fan and a maths enthusiast.
